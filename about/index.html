📄 About This Project

Hey there! 👋 Welcome to Data Center — a tiny project made with love by hamzah82. This place is basically just a lil' space on the web where I put random useful stuff that maybe (just maybe) could help someone out there 😄

No fancy stuff here. Just plain HTML, CSS, and a sprinkle of JavaScript. Everything runs on a simple list.json file. Yep, that’s it. Super lightweight. Super chill 🚴‍♂️

Wanna contribute? Feel free to click the Upload button on the homepage. It goes to a form — and if the file's useful and safe, I’ll add it manually. Nothing automatic here, all good vibes only 💌

Thanks for stopping by! I’m still learning and improving this thing slowly, so if you spot anything broken or weird, well... sorry in advance 🫣

Peace out! ✌️
